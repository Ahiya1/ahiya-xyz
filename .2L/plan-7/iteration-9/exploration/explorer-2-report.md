## Copy Tone Guidelines

### Voice Characteristics

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Confident | Boastful |
| Direct | Verbose |
| Expert | Arrogant |
| Specific | Vague |
| Results-focused | Process-obsessed |

### Example Transformations

**Generic (avoid):**
> "I use AI to build things faster."

**B2B Premium (use):**
> "Multi-agent orchestration compresses 8-week timelines to days. Built-in validation ensures quality doesn't suffer."

**Generic (avoid):**
> "Get in touch to discuss your project."

**B2B Premium (use):**
> "Ready to accelerate your next build? Let's talk specifics."
